| **Attribute** | **Description** |
|---------------|-----------------|
| **Name** | Multimedia Solutions Co. (Fictitious name) |
| **Sector** | Technology and Digital Marketing Services (Web Design, Graphic Design, Social Media Marketing) |
| **Mission** | Provide robust and creative digital solutions to small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) |
| **Infrastructure** | Internal network with web services, file servers, and a perimeter firewall as the primary defense |

---

## II. Security Incident Description

This incident is a forensic analysis and security improvement exercise based on a hypothetical volumetric attack scenario.

- **Event:** Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attack  
- **Vector:** Massive ICMP packet flood (Echo Request/ping)  
- **Exploited Vulnerability:** Misconfigured perimeter firewall (no rate-limiting policies or proper filtering of external ICMP traffic)  
- **Initial Impact:** Complete loss of internal network availability for two hours, interrupting critical business services  
- **Documentation Objective:** Apply the NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) to transform reactive findings into a proactive and sustainable security strategy
